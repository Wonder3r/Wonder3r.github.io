Travel Options

Air Travel

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. The island is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land within the next few years.

Cruise Ship

Some visitors arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.
