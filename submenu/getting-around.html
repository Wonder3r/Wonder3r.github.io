Ground Transportation

Rental Cars

Rental cars can be rented from a local agency near the airport.

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day.

Private Buses

Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis

Taxis are available in Taniti City.

Bike Rentals

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors.

Helmets are required by law.

Walkability of Taniti City

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable.

Many tourists explore the area surrounding Merriton Landing on foot.
